What We Do

Videography
& Editing

Footage that moves. Edits that land.

Great videography is about more than pressing record, it's about knowing where to be, when to move, and how to catch light in a way that makes the ordinary look extraordinary. Gen Production approaches every shoot with a cinematographer's eye and an editor's mind.

We shoot with professional cinemagrade equipment and edit with a strong sense of rhythm, colour and story. Whether you're handing us raw footage or bringing us in from the start, the end result is always a polished, cinematic video that serves its purpose.

This service covers pre-production to post-production or independently if you need just one.

From shoot to screen

01

Brief & Shot List

We discuss the project, the intended audience, and any must-have moments or shots. A detailed shot list or run-of-show is prepared so we know exactly what we're capturing.

02

Production

We show up with the right gear, at the right time, and move efficiently through the shoot — capturing clean, well-exposed footage that gives us options in the edit.

03

Rough Cut

A first assembly edit is shared for your review — focusing on structure, pacing and story. This is where we align on what the final piece will look and feel like before refinements begin.

04

Final Delivery

After revisions, the video is colour-graded, audio-mixed and exported in all required formats. Delivered via a private link or file transfer, ready to publish.
