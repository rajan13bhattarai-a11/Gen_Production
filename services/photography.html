How we work

01

Brief & Planning

We discuss the shoot goals, mood references, locations and any specific requirements. For portraits and commercial work, we provide a shoot plan so everyone arrives prepared.

02

The Shoot

We arrive early, set up deliberately, and create an environment where natural moments happen. For event work, we move efficiently and quietly. For portrait sessions, we take our time.

03

Culling & Selection

We review and select the strongest images from the shoot — quality over quantity. A curated gallery of selects is shared for your review before final retouching begins.

04

Retouching & Delivery

Final images are professionally colour-graded and retouched, then delivered as high-resolution files in the formats you need — print-ready, web-optimised, or both.
